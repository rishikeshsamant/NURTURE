Student Mental Health Resources

Resources

Meditation & Mindfulness Apps

Discover tools to help manage stress and improve mental well-being through mindfulness and meditation practices. These apps provide guided sessions for relaxation, focus, and emotional balance.

Peer Support Groups & Networks

Connect with others who understand your experiences through peer support groups. These groups offer a safe space for students to share challenges and provide mutual support.

Self-Help Tools and Apps

Access apps and online resources designed to help students manage mental health, track moods, and develop coping strategies. These tools offer practical ways to work on mental well-being independently.

Legal and Financial Support

Resources that provide legal aid, financial assistance, and guidance for students facing mental health challenges related to housing, discrimination, or financial stress.

Fitness & Physical Health Resources

Explore fitness programs, exercises, and routines that help support mental health. Physical activity is a great way to reduce stress and improve overall well-being.

Student Mental Health Stories & Testimonials

Real stories from students who have faced mental health challenges and have found ways to cope and thrive. These inspiring accounts can offer hope and practical advice.
